Play freely with ideas

Reshape MIDI patterns

Apply a variety of simple or complex variations to your MIDI clips with new MIDI Transformations. Add ornaments and articulations, draw acceletazion and deceleration curves, connect successive notes and chords, simulate the strum of a gutar, and more.

Generate inspiration

Conjure up melodies, chords and rhythms with the help of new MIDI Generators. Set constraints and let your chosen Generator create playful and original ideas for you tzo deleviop and turn into your own

Do more with Max for Live

Try out additional Max for Live Transformations and Generatorst that give you deeper ways of worrking with MIDI, or go even further by building new ones.

Embrace and explore tunings

Follow your preferred tuning system or try different ones more easily. Work outside the 12-tone equal temperament system, and access tunings with Live's devices as well as and MPE-capable plugin

Stay in key

Choose a scale in Live's Control Bar to see its notes in any MIDI clip you create. Then transform or generate ideas within the confines of the selected scale, or sync the scale of MIDI devices and instruments to the clip being played
